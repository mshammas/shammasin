Music

Music has always been a profound part of my life. Growing up in a musical family in Thalassery, Kerala, I was deeply influenced by the rich traditions of Indian melodies. My grandfather, the late TC Ummer, was a renowned musician and a guiding light in my musical journey. While I pursued a career in technology, my passion for music remained steadfast, leading me to explore and perform various genres, especially Ghazals and Hindustani music.
