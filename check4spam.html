What is Check4Spam?

Check4Spam is a free fact-checking service dedicated to identifying and exposing fake news, hoaxes, and misinformation circulating on social media. Founded in 2015 in Bengaluru, India, our mission is simple – make life easy for everyone and make life tough for spammers.

Created by tech enthusiasts Shammas Oliyath and Bal Krishn Birla, Check4Spam has grown into a trusted, community-driven platform that helps you decide if a viral message is true or false.

How It Works

  1. Submit a Forward or Claim: Send the suspicious message to our WhatsApp number or via our online submission form.
  2. We Investigate: Our team conducts thorough research by verifying data with trusted sources and through detailed online searches.
  3. Spam or Not Spam? We clearly label the claim as NOT SPAM (true) or SPAM (false) and provide a detailed report.
  4. See the Proof: Every verification post includes evidence like source links, images, and official statements with regular updates.

Key Features & Tools

Easy Submission

Forward any suspicious message via WhatsApp or submit it online. Fast, simple, and accessible to everyone.
