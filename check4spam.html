What is Check4Spam?

Check4Spam is a free fact-checking service dedicated to identifying and exposing fake news, hoaxes, and misinformation circulating on social media. Founded in 2015 in Bengaluru, India, our mission is simple – make life easy for everyone and make life tough for spammers. Just forward the suspicious message, and we’ll tell you whether it’s true or false.

Created by tech enthusiasts Shammas Oliyath and Bal Krishn Birla, Check4Spam has grown into a trusted, community-driven platform that ensures you share information confidently and safely.

How It Works

  1. Submit a Forward or Claim: Send a suspicious message to our WhatsApp number or use our online submission form.
  2. We Investigate: Our team digs deep by contacting official sources and performing detailed online research.
  3. Spam or Not Spam? We verify the claim and clearly label it as NOT SPAM (true) or SPAM (false) with a detailed report.
  4. See the Proof: Every post includes evidence such as source links, images, or official statements, with regular updates when needed.
